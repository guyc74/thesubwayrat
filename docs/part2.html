Manny moves¶

Subway obstacles¶

We drew Manny. We moved Manny.

Drawing obstacles and moving obstacles should very similar and therefor easy. The differences are: * The obstacle image is different. * Obstacles move from right to left and not up down. * The obstacles need to move without our control - even if we don’t touch the keyboard.
